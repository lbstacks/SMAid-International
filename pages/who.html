Vision

We envision a world in which individuals, families, and communities have the hope and capacity to build conditions that promote Total Health.

Mission

SMAid International promotes the Total Health of people living in the world’s poorest communities by partnering to:

  • Provide essential medicine
  • Promote community health development
  • Prevent and mitigate disease, disaster, and other health threats

We are stewards

As stewards, we understand the value of the talents bestowed upon us by our Lord and Savior Jesus. We take diligent care of what we have been entrusted with, extending our reach to the less privileged and marginalized. We are accountable for the resources generously given to us by donors.

Our Core Values

We believe that each individual’s work and efforts collectively help in achieving our results and targets. We hold each individual in high esteem, as well as the communities we work in/with. Furthermore, we believe in unity in diversity.

We are called to serve

As an organization, we believe we are called to serve the marginalized, and vulnerable with humility and Love. We also believe that as Christians we are called to provide support

We are people-centered

SMAid fosters holistic community development—mental, moral, physical, financial, and spiritual—without discrimination, focusing on lasting impact by prioritizing others' interests above our own

We are responsive

At SMAid, we swiftly respond to community appeals during emergencies and challenges, staying sensitive to changes and control mechanisms, always ready to act appropriately

We are accountable

At SMAid, we uphold transparency and accountability to God and donors. Resources are used as intended, with detailed annual reports provided to assure stakeholders of our integrity

We believe in teamwork

At SMAid, we value unity in diversity, holding individuals and communities in high esteem. Together, our collective efforts achieve shared goals, embodying one body with many parts

We are stewards

At SMAid, we steward God-given talents, skills, and resources to serve the marginalized, care for the land, and ensure donor contributions yield benefits for the communities we support.

Integrity is our hallmark

At SMAid, we act with integrity, honoring God and humanity. Guided by moral convictions, we ensure honesty, truthfulness, and consistency in all actions, plans, and circumstances.

On behalf of the Leadership, Management and Staff of Sustainable Mission Aid (SMAid) International, formerly Map International in Ghana, it is my pleasure to thank you for visiting our website and supporting the work we do in Ghana, Cote d’Ivoire and across Africa via our sisters NGOs partners in Liberia and Kenya.

SMAid International is a Health, Relief, and Development Organization that partners with vulnerable communities to improve the lives of Individuals, Children, and Families by identifying and helping them to overcome obstacles and challenges that prevent them from living life in its fullness.

Although SMAid International is a Christian organization, we are committed to helping and serving everyone without discrimination based on religion, gender, race, and other aspects of an individual’s background; as the cornerstone of our faith commands us in Mark 12:3: “Love your neighbour as yourself. There is no commandment greater than these”.
