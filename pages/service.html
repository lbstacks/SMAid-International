What We Do

Home / Service

SMAid International, a Christian health and development organization, is on a mission to transform the lives of people living in poverty-stricken conditions. With a remarkable 99% efficiency rating, SMAid responds to the needs of the underserved by providing life-saving medicines, disease prevention, and health promotion. Committed to holistic and sustainable development, SMAid empowers individuals with access to health, livelihood opportunities, and quality education, creating real hope and lasting change in communities worldwide.

Health Programs

SMAid is committed to improving healthcare accessibility and outcomes for vulnerable communities through essential medical supplies and awareness.

Education

SMAid believes education breaks the poverty cycle and unlocks human potential through access to quality and impactful educational programs.

Human Rights

Respecting and protecting human rights is at the core of SMAid’s mission. The organization works tirelessly to safeguard the rights and dignity of marginalized and vulnerable populations. Their human rights initiatives encompass advocacy, legal assistance, and community education.

And We Give It!

Our mission is unwavering to provide hope and support to those in need. We are dedicated to empowering individuals with access to essential health services, livelihood opportunities, quality education, and more. Through our holistic and sustainable approach, we strive to uplift underserved communities, making a real difference in their lives. Together, we can create a brighter and more promising future for all!

We believe that each individual’s work and efforts collectively help in achieving our results and targets. We hold each individual in high esteem, as well as the communities we work in/with.
